Frequently asked questions

How do I install BQ Wizard?

BQ Wizard is a Microsoft Excel add-in. After purchase, follow these steps:

  • Open Excel (Windows or Mac).
  • Go to Insert → Get Add-ins, then search for “BQ Wizard” in the Office Store, or install via the AppSource listing.
  • Open the BQ Wizard task pane from the Home tab in the Excel ribbon.
  • Enter your licence email and key to activate.

How do I activate my licence?

When BQ Wizard opens for the first time, you’ll see an activation screen. Enter the email you used at purchase and the licence key you received by email. Activation is instant and requires an internet connection on first run.

If activation fails, email us with the exact error message and the email address on your account.

Can I move my licence to a new computer?

Yes. Each BQ Wizard licence can be used on up to two devices. To move a licence to a third device, email ryan@bqwizard.com and we will release a device slot for you, usually within a few hours.

Where is my data stored?

By default, BQ Wizard stores all your libraries, dimension workings, take-off data and tool kits in your browser’s local storage on the machine you are using. Nothing leaves your computer unless cloud sync is enabled.

If you turn on cloud sync, your working data is encrypted and synced to your BQ Wizard account so it can follow you between devices. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

Does BQ Wizard work on Excel for Mac / Excel on the web / iPad?

BQ Wizard is built on the Office Add-in platform, so it runs inside Excel on Windows, Mac, the web and iPad. Some features (for example the PDF take-off tool and 3D view) work best on Windows or Mac desktop where there is more screen space and processing power.

I’ve found a bug or have a feature request.

We genuinely want to hear about both. Email ryan@bqwizard.com with as much detail as you can — screenshots, the file you were working on, and the steps that led to the problem. Feature requests from real users drive our roadmap.

How do I cancel my subscription?

If you purchased through Microsoft AppSource, cancel from your Microsoft account’s subscription page. If you purchased directly from bqwizard.com, email us and we will cancel and confirm by reply.

How do I request a refund?

Direct purchases from bqwizard.com can be refunded within 14 days under UK consumer law — see our Terms of Service. Microsoft AppSource purchases are refunded by Microsoft under the Microsoft Standard Contract.

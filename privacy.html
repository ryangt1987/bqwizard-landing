Privacy Policy

How the BQ Wizard add-in handles your information

Effective date: 23 April 2026

1. Scope

This privacy policy applies to the BQ Wizard add-in for Microsoft Excel (the “App”) and the related licence-activation and cloud-sync services operated on behalf of BQ Wizard. It does not cover third-party products that may be linked from the App or from the BQ Wizard website.

By installing or using the BQ Wizard App, you agree to the terms described below.

2. What data the App collects

BQ Wizard is designed to keep your estimating data on your own machine whenever possible. The App collects only what is needed to activate your licence and, if you choose, to sync your work between devices:

  • Licence activation. Your email address and licence key are transmitted to the BQ Wizard activation server when you activate the App.
  • Device identifier. The App creates a random device ID (UUID) on first run and stores it in your browser’s local storage. This is used to enforce the per-licence device limit described in the Terms of Service. It is not a hardware fingerprint and does not identify you personally.
  • Usage metadata. When the App checks your licence, the activation server records the timestamp and device ID so we can detect concurrent activations and provide support.
  • Cloud-sync content (optional). If you are signed in, BQ Wizard synchronises your libraries, dimension workings, PDF take-off measurements, and saved measurement tools to BQ Wizard cloud storage so you can continue your work on another device. This content is stored as an encrypted SQLite database linked to your account and is not shared with any third party.

The App does not contain advertising trackers, analytics SDKs, or telemetry about the drawings or spreadsheets you open. The contents of your PDF files and Excel workbooks are only transmitted to BQ Wizard servers if you explicitly use the cloud-sync feature.

3. What data the App does not collect

  • Passwords — BQ Wizard uses licence-key activation, not password logins.
  • Payment card details — purchases are processed by Microsoft AppSource or Stripe; BQ Wizard never sees your card.
  • The content of your spreadsheets or PDF drawings, unless cloud sync is enabled.
  • Browser history, cookies outside the App, or any information from other applications on your device.

4. How the data is used

  • To verify that your BQ Wizard licence is valid and within its device limit.
  • To provide the cloud-sync service when you opt in.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To detect abuse, such as a single licence being shared across many machines.

BQ Wizard does not sell your data, rent it to third parties, or use it for advertising.

5. Data retention

  • Licence records (email, licence key, device IDs, activation history) are kept for as long as your licence is active and for up to twelve months after it expires, for accounting and anti-fraud purposes.
  • Cloud-sync content is kept while your account is active. When you delete your account, the content is removed from our active systems within 30 days and from backups within 90 days.
  • Support emails are kept for up to two years.

6. Third parties

BQ Wizard uses the following service providers, each of which is subject to its own privacy policy:

  • Cloudflare — hosting of this website and the activation API.
  • Microsoft — distribution of BQ Wizard through the Microsoft AppSource marketplace, including Microsoft-operated purchase and billing flows.
  • Stripe — processing of direct payments from bqwizard.com (if applicable).

No other third parties receive personal data from the App.

7. Your rights

Under UK and EU data-protection law you have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data held about you.
  • Correct data that is inaccurate.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to processing, or ask us to restrict it.
  • Export your cloud-sync content as a downloadable file.

To exercise any of these rights, email ryan@bqwizard.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

Communications between the BQ Wizard App and its servers use HTTPS/TLS. Cloud-sync content is stored in encrypted form at rest. Access to production systems is restricted and audited. No system can be perfectly secure, but BQ Wizard takes reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data.

9. Children

BQ Wizard is a professional tool for the construction industry and is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page and the effective date above will be revised. Material changes will be announced inside the App on next launch.

11. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy, or about BQ Wizard’s handling of your data, can be sent to ryan@bqwizard.com.

BQ Wizard is operated as a sole trader based in the United Kingdom.
